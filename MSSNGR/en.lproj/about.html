MSSNGR is a guest communication app for hotels to let their guests decide about what, when and where they want to be informed. MSSNGR is an easy to use iPhone app which delivers information about activities and events right to the iPhone of the hotel guest. All information is automatically filtered by the personal and individual interests of each hotel guest. With MSSNGR, no important information is lost on its way to the guest, MSSNGR ensures all guests receive only information they really care about. Also, everything to know about the hotel is delivered right to the iPhone by MSSNGR - always up to date and easy to use.
